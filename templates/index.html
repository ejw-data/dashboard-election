Election Voting Trends

Every 10 years...

the United States Census determines the number of electoral votes for each state and the number of US House of Representatives members will be sent to Washington. The determination of the district boundaries are drawn by each state, which leads to interesting shapes with unique characteristics.

On this page you will find a variety of information about the districts of each state and how they are unique from one and another and how this may influence current voting trends.

Gerrymandering...

Creating favorable districts for a specific political party is commonly brought to federal courts and just by viewing the odd shapes makes one question the fair distribution.

With the nation polarized and politicians closely aligning their actions with their home district's beliefs, one must wonder if the districts have been drawn on ideological basis; thus, reinforcing isolated political views.
