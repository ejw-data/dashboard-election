District Maps...

Below is the district map for the 114th Congress. This congressional session began January 3, 2015 and adjourned January 3, 2017. The members elected to this congress were elected the term prior to the 2016 Presidential elections, and provided insights about the general direction that the country was headed as shown in the scatter plot on this page.

When compared to the congressional district map of the 107th Congress, a significant difference is clearly visible. The 107th Congress began January 3, 2001 and adjourned January 3, 2003. This congress had the most complete information and had similar characteristics with the 2016 election year -- a narrow Republican presidential victory, recovery from a recession, rapidly ballooning national debt, and many other similar issues.

Gerrymandering...

Creating favorable districts for a specific political party is commonly brought to federal courts, and simply by viewing the odd shapes makes one question the fair distribution. With the nation polarized and politicians closely aligning their actions with their home district's beliefs, one must wonder if the districts have been drawn on ideological basis; thus, reinforcing isolated political views.
